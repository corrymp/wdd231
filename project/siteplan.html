Name: MC Player Resource

Domain: https://mc-player-resource.com

Reason

Mojang policy prevents using "Minecraft", but "MC" is okay to use.

The site aims to be a resources for easily viewing Minecraft player information.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to allow users to view public information on select Minecraft players. The site offers tools to view public information on players in an easy-to-consume format, add new players to their personal list, and get a better view of what those players currently look like in the game.

Scenarios

  • A fan of Minecraft-focused YouTubers wants to see their favorite players in 3D.
  • An avid Minecraft player is interested in viewing characters and names of their friends in one place.
  • A Minecraft server administrator wants to quickly check if a troublemaker has even been punished by Mojang.
